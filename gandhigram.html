About Gandhigram

Gandhigram Trust works to enhance the socio-economic status of village communities through interventions in sectors like health and education following Gandhian principles. The outreach programs are aimed at promoting the establishment of village industries and providing rehabilitation to those in need. The Trust overviews 16 organisations in the field of Education, Healthcare, Community Development and Rehabilitation, listed below:

  • Soundaram Vidyalaya
  • Gandhigram Primary School
  • Lakshmi college of education
  • Thambihottam higher secondary school
  • Dr. Soundram Trust
  • Kasturba Hospital
  • Lakshmi Seva Sangham
  • Kasturba Sevikashram Santi School for deaf and dumb
  • Avvai Ashram
  • Soubhagya Illam
  • Khadi and Village Industries
  • Public Charitable Trust
  • Village Extension Program

Primary Research

To study the functioning by the trust, we travelled to Gandhigram and Sivasailam to observe and interview the people who were managing different sectors. We received help from Subramanyam, who helped translate and guided me as well. We also talked with the people who were working in different sectors of the trust. On interviewing an artisan we found that his family had been with the trust for three generations. He also told how he studied in the school of trust itself and continued the profession after his father. After noting down the information and interpreting the interviews we were able to categorise the work done by different sectors of the trust.
